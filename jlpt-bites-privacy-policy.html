JLPT Bites - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

JLPT Bites does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app operates fully on-device and does not transmit user information to external servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or behavior tracking

Subscriptions & Purchases

If you choose to subscribe through Google Play, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store any financial or personal information about you.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the app only with the supervision of a parent or guardian.

Third-Party Services

JLPT Bites does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
